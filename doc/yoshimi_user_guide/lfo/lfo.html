LFOs

We only show the amplitude LFO as all of these have identical controls.

  • Freq. The frequency of the oscillator
  • Depth The amplitude variation applied
  • Start (phase) The LFO position when the note starts
  • Delay Time delay before the LFO comes in to effect
  • Rand Amp. Degree of 'humanising' variation of LFO amplitude
  • Rand Freq. Degree of 'humanising' variation of LFO frequency
  • Str. (stretch) Anticlockwise LFO is faster on lower pitch notes and vice-versa When BPM?
  • BPM When checked locks the delay control to the incoming MIDI clock
  • Cont. Normally the LFO starts and stops with the notes, but when checked the LFO is free running independent of whether any notes are sounding. When BPM?
  • Type The shape of the LFO waveform
