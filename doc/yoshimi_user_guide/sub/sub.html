SubSynth

SubSynth is the simplest engine from the point view of the number of controls. It is a single view of (in essence) a broadband noise generator with a series of harmonics filters that only allow comparatively narrow bands through to the output. There are also the following standardised inserts. Most of these are initially disabled.
