Add/Pad Waveforms

This waveform editor is used for both AddSynth and PadSynth.

Note: PadSynth doesn't have the Phase or Harmonic Randomness controls. Instead it has the Apply button.

The bank of sliders controls 128 individual harmonics. The top row being amplitude, and the bottm row, relative phase.
